# Why We Built Lumis

There's a gap in mental health support that affects nearly everyone. 60% of people who need help never seek it — not because help doesn't exist, but because the barriers are too high. Stigma. Cost. Waitlists. The fear of being judged.

We built Lumis because we believe that gap doesn't have to exist.

## The Problem We Saw

Traditional therapy is powerful. But it has structural limitations that leave millions of people without support:

- **Cost:** Therapy sessions average $150-250 per session. BetterHelp costs $260-400/month. For many people, that's simply not accessible.
- **Availability:** Therapists have limited hours. Your worst moment might come at 2 AM on a Tuesday — when no one is available.
- **Stigma:** Despite progress, 60% of people who need mental health support avoid seeking it due to stigma. The word "therapy" itself is a barrier.
- **Continuity:** Even with a great therapist, there are 167 hours between weekly sessions. That's 167 hours where insights fade, motivation drops, and old patterns reassert themselves.

## What We Believe

We believe that AI — done right — can bridge this gap. Not replace therapy, but fill the spaces where nothing exists today.

Imagine support that:

- **Remembers your story** across every conversation, so session 100 feels like session 100
- **Is there at 2 AM** when you can't sleep and your mind won't stop racing
- **Costs less than a coffee subscription** while using the same evidence-based techniques as professional therapists
- **Notices patterns you can't see** — like the connection between your Sunday anxiety and Monday meetings
- **Follows up on what you committed to** — gently, without judgment

That's Lumis.

## Why "Growth Companion" — Not "AI Therapist"

We made a deliberate choice not to call Lumis an AI therapist. Not because the techniques aren't therapeutic — they are (CBT, DBT, ACT, Motivational Interviewing, all evidence-based). But because:

1. **Framing matters.** "Therapy" activates stigma for 60% of our potential users. "Personal growth" activates aspiration. Same destination, different door.
2. **Honesty matters.** Lumis is an AI. It can't replace the human connection of a therapist. But it can offer things a therapist can't: infinite patience, perfect memory, 24/7 availability, and zero judgment.
3. **Access matters.** By positioning as a growth companion, we reach people who would never download a "therapy app" — but who desperately need support.

## What Makes Lumis Different

The AI mental health space is growing. What makes us different is one word: **memory**.

Most AI chatbots reset every session. They don't know your story, your triggers, your relationships, or what techniques have helped you before. Every conversation starts from zero.

Lumis has a 4-tier memory system that grows with you:

1. **Session summaries** — what happened in each conversation
2. **Semantic embeddings** — deep understanding of your themes and patterns
3. **Structured memories** — your goals, relationships, triggers, and coping strategies
4. **Relationship mapping** — how the people in your life affect your wellbeing

This means Lumis gets better the longer you use it. Session 50 is fundamentally more valuable than session 1 — because by then, Lumis understands you.

## Our Commitment

We're building Lumis with three non-negotiable principles:

1. **Privacy first.** Your conversations are encrypted and never sold. Period.
2. **Safety always.** Crisis detection and professional resource referral are built in at every level. Crisis support is always free.
3. **Honesty about what we are.** Lumis is an AI companion, not a human therapist. We'll always be transparent about our capabilities and limitations.
