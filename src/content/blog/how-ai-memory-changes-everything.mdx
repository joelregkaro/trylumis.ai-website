# How AI Memory Makes Therapy Support Different

Most AI chatbots have a dirty secret: they don't actually know you. Every conversation starts from scratch. You tell the same story again. You re-explain your triggers. You lose the thread of insights from last week.

This isn't just inconvenient — it's fundamentally incompatible with how personal growth works.

## Why Memory Matters

Real therapeutic change happens over time, not in isolated moments. A good therapist doesn't just listen — they remember. They connect this week's anxiety to last month's relationship conflict. They notice that your mood dips every Sunday evening. They recall the coping strategy that worked for you in March and suggest it again in October.

Without memory, AI support is like having a new therapist every session. You get the techniques, but you lose the context that makes those techniques powerful.

## How Lumis Remembers

Lumis uses a 4-tier memory architecture designed to mirror how human memory works:

### Tier 1: Session Summaries

After each conversation, Lumis creates a structured summary: what you discussed, what emotions came up, what insights emerged, and what you committed to. This is like a therapist's session notes.

### Tier 2: Semantic Embeddings

Your conversations are transformed into mathematical representations (embeddings) stored in a vector database. This allows Lumis to find relevant past conversations based on meaning, not just keywords. When you mention feeling overwhelmed at work, Lumis can recall the three other times you felt that way — and what helped.

### Tier 3: Structured Memories

Key facts about you — your goals, relationships, triggers, coping strategies, milestones — are extracted and maintained as structured data. This is your personal knowledge base that Lumis references in every conversation.

### Tier 4: Relationship Mapping

The people in your life affect your wellbeing in patterns. Lumis tracks these relationships and how they correlate with your emotional states. This enables insights like: "I've noticed conversations about your manager tend to come up before your anxiety spikes."

## The Compound Effect

Here's what makes memory transformative: **it creates a compound effect.**

- Week 1: Lumis learns your basic story.
- Week 4: Lumis starts noticing patterns you didn't see.
- Week 12: Lumis can predict challenges and proactively support you.
- Week 24: Lumis has a deep, nuanced understanding of your emotional landscape.

Each week of data makes the next week's insights sharper. This is why we say Lumis "gets more valuable over time" — it's not a marketing phrase, it's architecturally true.

## Privacy by Design

More memory means more responsibility. That's why every layer of our memory system is built with privacy at its core:

- All data is encrypted at rest and in transit
- Row-level security ensures your data is only accessible to you
- We never use your conversations to train general AI models
- You can view, export, or delete everything Lumis knows about you at any time

Your memories are yours. We just help you use them to grow.
